CIS 486: Overview on assignments and Exams

Capstone Project:

Students will complete a real-world, comprehensive IT-based project that incorporates the tools, technologies, and methodologies from their coursework to date. A successful project includes meeting project scope criteria, quality and validation standards, and the inclusion of project and system documentation that ensure subsequent project iterations.

Make-Up Examinations/Work:

  • University approved and valid absences must be provided and approved in advance via Canvas Inbox.

Exceptions:

  • Extensions - Considered on a case-by-case basis and require pre-approval
  • Extra Credit - Additional extra credit is given at the discretion of the instructor and are considered only for students who have submitted all other bonus assignments

Grade Disputes:

  • Please submit possible grading errors or disputes via Canvas Inbox within one week of the grade posting.
  • Please include all pertinent information including the specific assignment, question number & text, instructions, textbook reference, screenshot, or other.
  • If you do include a screenshot, also transcribe any information into text.
  • Do not submit via submission comments.
